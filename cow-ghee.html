A fruit variety of Thailand , Apple ber is sweet , juicy and has plenty of medicinal properties.The plum resembles an apple in its appearance and taste.Hence is known as Apple Ber.It is a new type of fruit now available in market.We grow annually more than 25 tones of Apple Ber to cater to the domestic and international market.

Growing Conditions

The plant starts yielding fruits after 6 months and can grow well in varied climatic Conditions. It can grow well in semi-arid conditions with standing high exposure to sunlight. The tree yields fruit once a year.
